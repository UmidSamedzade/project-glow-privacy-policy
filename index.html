Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2025

Thank you for choosing Project Glow. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect

Project Glow uses a guest account system. You can customize the name of your guest account, but formal registration is not required to play the game.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your game account
  • To authenticate and secure your account
  • To communicate important updates or support messages

3. Unity Ads

To enhance your game experience, we use Unity Ads. You can watch ads to continue playing after each loss. We do not show other types of ads in the game. Unity Ads may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policy.

4. Analytics

We use Google Play Console for analytics to improve the game's performance, fix issues, and enhance your experience. Play Console may collect information such as your device type, game activity, and usage patterns.

5. LootLocker

We use LootLocker to store your account data. This includes your guest account information and game progress essential for the game's functionality. LootLocker follows industry best practices to keep your data secure. You can learn more about LootLocker's data practices at their privacy policy.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Update: You can update your username through the game's settings or by contacting us at the email address provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Project Glow is intended for users of all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from your child without proper consent, please contact us immediately at the email address below.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
